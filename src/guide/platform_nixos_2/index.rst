Platform: NixOS 19.03/20.09
===========================

Our third-generation platform is based upon `NixOS 19.03 <http://nixos.org/>`_
and NixOS 20.09.

NixOS provides `novel approaches <https://nixos.org/features.html>`_ for
installing and managing software.
